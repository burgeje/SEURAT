SEURAT - Software Engineering Using RATionale

SEURAT is an Eclipse plug-in to capture and use design rationale.

Design rationale consistes of the reasons for decisions made when designing/developing software or any other artifact. SEURAT supports the following capabilities:

  • Allows entry of requirements, decisions, alternatives, arguments, assumptions procedures, questions, and answers
  • Links decision alternatives to the code that implements them
  • Inferences over the rationale to detect inconsistencies
  • Incorporates Pattern and Tactic libraries to assist in selecting patterns and tactics based on the non-functional requirements for your system and to automatically generate rationale corresponding to patterns and tactics
  • Integrates with the XFeature plug-in to support feature modeling for product line development

More information

SEURAT was originally developed at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. Development has continued at Miami University. More information about SEURAT, including a list of design rationale-related publications, can be found at http://www.users.miamioh.edu/burgeje/SEURAT/index.html.
